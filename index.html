About me

I received the B.S. degree in Automation from School of Control and Computer Engineering, North China Electric Power University (NCEPU) in 2019. I am now the 2nd year master majored in Control Engineering from School of Automation, Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT), under the supervision of Prof. Zehua Guo.
